About Us

Discover our mission, values, and the people who make Victory Baptist Church a family

Welcome to Victory Baptist Church

Victory Baptist Church has been a beacon of faith and community in Kittanning for many years. We are a Bible-believing church committed to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing believers in their faith, and reaching out to our community with the love of Christ.

Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples who love Him with all their hearts, serve Him faithfully, and share His truth with others. We believe in the authority of Scripture, the power of prayer, and the importance of fellowship among believers.

At Victory Baptist Church, you'll find a warm and welcoming congregation that values authentic worship, biblical teaching, and genuine relationships. Whether you're seeking answers to life's big questions, looking for a church family, or wanting to grow deeper in your faith, we invite you to join us.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone through faith alone in Jesus Christ alone, and not by works.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and evangelism.
